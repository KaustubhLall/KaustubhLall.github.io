What Dusk is

ResMed now publicly describes Dusk as a free, private, personal, and empathetic sleep-support coach. It offers natural hands-free voice support, answers questions day or night, and adapts its guidance to how someone feels, sleeps, and moves through everyday life.

The product creates personalized CBT-I plans and turns sleep and behavior expertise into practical daily guidance. ResMed is equally explicit about the boundary: Dusk is for general wellness and information, not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Architecture (capability level)

An agent-based architecture with specialized agents for distinct responsibilities, retrieval-augmented generation over curated knowledge, custom persistent user-specific memory, LangChain orchestration, speech-to-text and text-to-speech for full voice interaction, and a complete supporting backend.

IP pending

The multi-agent CBT-I system is associated with a pending intellectual-property filing, publicly listed as P2852US1 (P+S reference 157222). This is a pending filing reference, not a patent grant. The public listing corroborates the multi-agent CBT-I direction without disclosing the implementation.

Public sources

The product description, signup availability, voice support, personalized CBT-I plans, and general-wellness boundary come from ResMed's official Dusk page.
